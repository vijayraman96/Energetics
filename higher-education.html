Duke University

Apple Student ID. 
The one‑tap wonder.

Duke University uses Apple Student ID to help students, faculty, staff and alumni get around campus. Once they add their student ID to Apple Wallet, they can use iPhone or Apple Watch to enter dorms and libraries, ride the campus bus and pay for meals. And it’s not just convenient — Apple Student ID keeps all their information private and secure.
